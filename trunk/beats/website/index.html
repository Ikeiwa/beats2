Beats - Beta Release:

"Beats, Advance Rhythm Game for Android" is a music-based rhythm game where you tap to the beat of your favourite song. If you have ever played StepMania, DJ Max, Beatmania, or Dance Dance Revolution (DDR), you should feel very much at home.*

Note: You must DOWNLOAD YOUR OWN StepMania songs/stepfiles!

Note: Beats currently does not support .dwi files so if your song pack uses .dwi files, you must convert them to .sm files using StepMania. See the StepMania website for instructions.

Installing Stepfiles:

For this beta release, you must manually install the songs. To do this:
1) Connect phone to computer
2) Download a song pack in .zip/.rar/.smzip format.
3) Use a file archiver to extract the pack's content to your computer
4) Copy the folders into your phone's "Beats/Songs" folder
5) Disconnect your phone and run Beats on your phone
6) Use "Select Music" to navigate to your song's folder and select a stepfile ending with '.sm'
7) Press MENU button to select stepfile difficulty and press the 'Start' to play your song
